Electrical Upgrades in Rockwall

If you are facing a challenge when it comes to your electrical service or are considering a service upgrade to support a future project, let our experts lend a hand by providing a free consultation. We are committed to providing cost-free estimates and assessments for our customers so that they are aware of all of the possible hazards and options in this important decision.

We will ensure that your installation will meet or exceed the most current version of the National Electrical Code so that you can have peace of mind knowing that your safety and insurance compliance is well in hand.

Are you unsure about whether you need to alter your electrical service? As part of our free estimate, we can walk you through a personalized load analysis to let you know definitively where you stand in electrical usage and future need so that you can accurately plan your next move. Call today!
